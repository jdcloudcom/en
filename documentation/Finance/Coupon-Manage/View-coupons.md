# **View Coupon**

### **1. How to view coupon amount?**

Login JD Cloud Home page [www.jdcloud.com](https://www.jdcloud.com/), click the login button in the upper right corner and enter the JD account to log in to enter the Cost-Coupon to view the coupon amount of the account.

### **2. How do view the use bill of coupon?**

Login JD Cloud Home page [www.jdcloud.com](https://www.jdcloud.com/), click the login button in the upper right corner and enter the JD account to log in to enter the Cost-Coupon, then click Used or Expired Coupon column and click **Search Bill** to view the use bill of coupon.![4.png](http://img1.jcloudcs.com/cms/372b453e-489a-4020-8f5a-ef534338e30520170818161549.png)

### **3. How to view list of available coupon?**

Login JD Cloud Home page [www.jdcloud.com](https://www.jdcloud.com/), click the login button in the upper right corner and enter the JD account to log in to enter the Cost-Coupon, then click Unused and Used to view the available coupon list.
